Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

We collect nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels, no advertising identifiers, no server-side storage of anything about you.

What stays on your device

Your tournament progress, squad, and settings are saved in your browser's localStorage on your own device. They never leave it. Clearing your browser data deletes them. If you install the game to your home screen, a service worker caches the game files on your device so it works offline — that cache contains game assets, not personal data.

Hosting

The static files of the Game are served by our hosting provider, which may process your IP address in transient server logs as a technical necessity of serving any website. We do not access, store, or analyse these.

Donations

If you choose to donate, that happens entirely on Ko-fi's website under Ko-fi's privacy policy. No payment or identity information ever reaches the Game.

Children

The Game collects no data from anyone, including children.
